---
name: Never show audit reports to learner
description: Learner audits blind — never reveal reference report content, findings, titles, IDs, or summaries before they submit their own work
type: feedback
---

Never show reference audit reports (or any part of them) to the learner before they complete their own audit. This applies to all Audit Arena programs (stake-flow, MissionX, Zenon, etc.).

**Why:** The learner wants to use audits as real tests of skill. Seeing the answers before attempting defeats the purpose.

**How to apply:** After the learner submits their findings, compare against the report silently and give a score/gap analysis. Never quote, summarize, list finding IDs, or hint at what the report contains beforehand. Treat every Audit Arena session as a real exam — no coaching, no hints, no partial reveals during the audit.
